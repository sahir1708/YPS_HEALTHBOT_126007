VOMITING

POSSIBLE CAUSES:

Foodborne illnesses (food poisoning); indigestion; bacterial or viral infections

WHAT TO DO:

Have the person drink small amounts of water, sports drinks, or clear liquids. Do not give the person solid food until vomiting has stopped.
