COUGHING

POSSIBLE CAUSES:

Irritants such as smoke, dust; improperly swallowing food or water

WHAT TO DO:

Drink extra water. Avoid exposure to allergens and irritants. Dry, tickling coughs can be relieved by sucking on cough lozenges or hard candy. For nasal stuffiness, try saline nasal spray. Increasing humidity in the air helps relieve a cough
