CUTS

WHAT TO DO:

Small cut:

Wash the cut with water and apply antiseptic. Minor cuts and scrapes usually stop bleeding on their own.

Deep cut:

Apply gentle pressure with a clean bandage or cloth and elevate the wound until bleeding stops. Keep the wound under running tap water to reduce the risk of infection. Apply a thin layer of an antibiotic ointment, apply a bandage, rolled gauze or gauze held in place with paper tape. Cover the wound to keep it clean. Rush to the hospital if cut appears very severe.
