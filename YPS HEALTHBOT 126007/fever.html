FEVER

POSSIBLE CAUSES:

Over exertion of the body; Pain; Stress; Or disease

WHAT TO DO:

Large volume of fluid intake and applying a damp cloth on the forehead. If the fever is 102 or higher: Give an over-the-counter medicine such as acetaminophen ( Tylenol) or ibuprofen ( Advil, Motrin) as directed on the label. If fever persists take to hospitals
